Kechun Xu

I am a fourth-year PhD (Sep. 2021 - ) student in Control Science and Engineering department at Zhejiang University. I belong to Robotics Lab, advised by Prof. Rong Xiong and Yue Wang. I am currently a visiting student at MSC Lab in UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka. I also spent time in Alibaba Cloud and ZJ Humanoid as a research intern.

I obtained my B.Eng (Sep. 2017 - Jun. 2021) in Control Science and Engineering and Mechanical and Electronic Engineering from Zhejiang University with an honor degree at Chu Kochen Honor College.

My current research interests lie in robotic manipulation, robot learning, and embodied AI. My research goal is to develop human-level intelligence in robotic manipulation, including dexterity (of human-like behaviors), efficiency (in planning and learning), and generalization (to open scenarios).

I am now open to potential academic and industry job opportunities. Please feel free to contact me!
